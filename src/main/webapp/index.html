Liberating app development

What is Love Lyndir?

Love Lyndir is a campaign aimed at providing all of Lyndir's apps free of charge to users, and under open source licenses to developers, allowing maximum benefit from them while giving everyone the freedom to contribute as they see fit.

Developing quality apps takes a lot of time and effort. Traditionally, this effort comes at the expense of users and progress. People are forced to contribute before even seeing an app or experience using it. People with development skills are denied the details about how apps work and the means of fixing problems with them. Apps are stuck in a "proprietory jail" which limits exposure and progress.

Love Lyndir is an attempt at breaking free from this jail. Anyone interested in a Lyndir app can get it immediately and free of charge. All apps are fully functional and usable. All apps are also open source, essentially providing the schematics of the internals to anyone skilled enough to use them to improve or resolve issues. Anyone who appreciates the app's contribution to their lives can in turn contribute to the future development of all Lyndir apps.

Love Lyndir is a campaign by Maarten Billemont, Lyndir (© 2013).
